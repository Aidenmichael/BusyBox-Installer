<i>BusyBox Installer</i> is a one-click BusyBox installation utility for various android architectures (ARM/ARM64 and x86).

<b>WARNING: I Am NOT Responsible for any Damages on Your Device!</b>

BusyBox Installer requires <b>ROOT</b> Access.

<b>Features</b>

* An easy BusyBox installation for ARM/ARM64 and x86.
* Removal of BusyBox applets (only) installed by this app.
* Show applets list (only) installed by this app.
* Dark and Light themes (depends on system theme).
